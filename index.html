Kazi Zehad Mostofa – Bio

Hi! I’m Kazi Zehad Mostofa

Research Assistant || Load Forecasting | Microgrid Optimisation | Net Zero Building Energy Design |

I am a Ph.D. researcher at Clarkson University specializing in deterministic and probabilistic load forecasting, renewable integration, and sustainable infrastructure. With strong skills in machine learning, IoT platforms, and energy modeling, I’ve led projects on smart buildings, hybrid energy systems, and resilient microgrids.

Currently, I am working on microgrid optimization for Tok, Alaska—an initiative in collaboration with the U.S. Department of Energy (DoE) and GE Vernova—aimed at delivering reliable, cost-effective energy solutions to remote communities.

To explore my recent work, I would like to welcome you to visit my profiles in Google Scholar & LinkedIn.
