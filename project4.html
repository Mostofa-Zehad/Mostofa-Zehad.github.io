Hybrid PV and Low-E Glass Retrofit for Sustainable School Buildings in Bangladesh

This project demonstrates a combined approach of rooftop PV systems and Low-E coated windows for improving the energy efficiency, thermal comfort, and sustainability of school buildings across Bangladesh. It applies a hybrid design methodology validated through simulation tools such as HOMER Pro and HelioScope.

1. Problem Statement & Objectives

Most schools in Bangladesh suffer from poor thermal insulation and high daytime energy demand, leading to dependency on inefficient grid systems or diesel generators. This project proposes a retrofitting solution using PV and Low-E technologies to reduce load and emissions.
